Printing inks and coatings are not only crucial for branding, appearance, and functionality of a packaging, but also for creating circular packaging solutions. Their technical functionalities can actively support all three levers of a Circular Economy: Reduction, Reuse and Recycling. This informative webinar features Siegwerk experts discussing the potential for inks to create the packaging solutions necessary to move from a linear to a Circular Economy.
