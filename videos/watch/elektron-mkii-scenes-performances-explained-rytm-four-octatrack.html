The Elektron Octatrack, Analog Rytm and Four are known for their creative way of going about step sequencing. The MKII series all allow real-time modulation control over synthesis and effects parameters. This is a function that makes the instruments ”playable” even though one might have prepared patterns with both rhythm and melody in beforehand.

In this video I’ll explore how Performances and Scenes are handled with the Rytm, Four and Octatrack MKII.
