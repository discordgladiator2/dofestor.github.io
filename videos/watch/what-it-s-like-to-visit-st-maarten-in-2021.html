This is what it’s like to visit St. Maarten		 St. Martin in 2021.

St. Maarten/St. Martin has recently re-opened to International visitors and is welcoming more and more travelers each week. What’s it like to visit St. Maarten in 2021? In this video, we’ll explain what our experience was like, where we stayed and what activities you don’t want to miss while visiting St. Maarten on a land vacation, or by cruise ship. We’ll also cover the pre-travel testing and insurance requirements and talk about health and safety on the Friendly Island.
