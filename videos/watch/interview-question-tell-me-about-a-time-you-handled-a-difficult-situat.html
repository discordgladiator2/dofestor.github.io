This common interview question is sometimes asked in the following manner:
Q. What have you done in the past when you were faced with a difficult situation?
Q. How have you solved a difficult problem at work?
Q. Tell me about a challenging problem you faced in your previous position?
Q. Describe a difficult task and how you dealt with it?

HOW TO ANSWER THE INTERVIEW QUESTION TELL ME ABOUT A TIME YOU HANDLED A DIFFICULT SITUATION.
JOB INTERVIEW TIP #1 – Give a previous difficult work situation you were in that demonstrates you are a DETERMINED, positive, and resilient person.

JOB INTERVIEW TIP #2 – In your answer, show you are someone who always puts the needs of your team first!

JOB INTERVIEW TIP #3 – Show you are not afraid to tackle difficult situations professionally!
