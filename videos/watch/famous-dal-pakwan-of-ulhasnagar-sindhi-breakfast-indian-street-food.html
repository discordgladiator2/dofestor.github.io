Yet again, we are back in Ulhasnagar. This time we have covered the best sindhi street food place in Ulhasnagar called Doli Nasta house. This place is known for the Dal pakvan ‚Äì A Sindhi dish that is lot spoken about. While whole of the city has the stalls that serve dal pakvan early morning, this one has a specific different taste that immediately develops your fondness for it. Dal Pakvan is a dish that has Chana dal served with Pakvan ‚Äì a crisp fried maida roti along with green chutney and the sour tamarind chutney.

Address: Doli Nasta House, Near Ulhasnagar Station (W) ,Ulhasnagar - 3
