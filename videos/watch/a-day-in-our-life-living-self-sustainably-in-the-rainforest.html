Some of the small steps we take to live self sufficiently and with zero waste bring so much meaning to our lives. They help us on our journey to regenerate the land and repair it for generations to come. https://www.patreon.com/motherthemountain

Join us for a typical day on the farm, where we care for the animals, harvest and preserve abundant fruit, and work hard planting, fixing and building.

We acknowledge the Traditional Custodians of the Country we live on, and recognise their continuing connection to the land and waters.
