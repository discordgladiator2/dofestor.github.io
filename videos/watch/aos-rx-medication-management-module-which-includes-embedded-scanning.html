AOS Rx was designed to achieve the following outcomes:

 Improve narcotic discrepancy, which includes drugs dispensed, administered, returned and wasted;
 Improve the tracking of compound drugs;
 Improve the real-time inventory refills of medication stations.
 Leverage advanced analytics to assist with detection of drug diversion;
 Reduce costs associated with narcotic reconciliation;
 Reduce costs associated with detecting drug diversion;
 Reduce costs associated with billing by sending all desired discrete billing data to billing system and eliminate the labor costs associated with paper related workflows;
 Improve the charge capture of meds and supplies used in the perioperative suite;
 Improve patient safety by scanning in patient information; and
 Improve patient safety by running drug/drug and drug/allergy interaction checks once the meds are scanned.
