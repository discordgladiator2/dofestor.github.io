This is the music video for the song ‘Trial and Error’ by dutch rock band Face Tomorrow. Trial and Error is the second single of Face Tomorrow’s third album ‘In The Dark’, out now in the Netherlands on Excelsior Recordings.

Main character: Rena Noordermeer
Producer: Marc Nolte
