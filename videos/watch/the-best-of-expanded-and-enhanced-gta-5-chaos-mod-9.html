Everything amazing in the new Expanded and Enhanced Chaos Mod! Now with cutscenes and on-screen chat. This Grand Theft Auto 5 mod now has over 300 effects and include some meta effects that vastly change how the effects are applied. Viewers now control the likelihood of any of the 4 effects occuring, so if 60% vote for A and 40% for B, that is their chance of occurring. This keeps things somewhat random but viewers still have a say. Everything from Doomsday, Beyblades, Brad coming back to life, Aliens, to rainbow vehicles. I have held world records in all GTA V speedrun categories at one time or another and its nice to find something to change up things so drastically. This mod has also been called a ‘cheat mod’ or ‘Chaos%’ due to is similarities with a cheat possible in Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas. Glitches and oddities can occur, but its all a part of the fun.
