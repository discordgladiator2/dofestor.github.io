In this meeting we discussed about ContinualAI collaborative projects and how you can join them. In particular we will focus on the "Avalanche" one: an End-to-End Library for Continual Learning.

The schedule for this meetup will be:

- Introduction to Avalanche - Vincenzo Lomonaco, President of ContinualAI
- Benchmarks: Generate your Data Stream at Ease - Lorenzo Pellegrini, University of Bologna
- Training: Fast Prototyping of CL Algorithms - Antonio Carta, University of Pisa
- Evaluation Metrics & Loggers - Andrea Cossu, University of Pisa
- Models Zoo and How to Contribute - Gabriele Graffieti, University of Bologna

Moderated by Keiland Cooper.

More info and slides here: https://continualai.discourse.group/t/continualai-meetup-avalanche-an-end-to-end-library-for-continual-learning/252
