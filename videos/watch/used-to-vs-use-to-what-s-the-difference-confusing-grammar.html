The phrases "used to" and "use to" are two of the most confusing phrases in English. But I’m going to break this down for you and help you understand the difference as well as talk about why learners may have trouble with these phrases.

And even if you didn’t use to know the differences, or perhaps you just weren’t used to using these phrases in everyday conversation, I’m confident that by the end of this lesson you will have gotten used to these confusing phrases and have a perfect understanding of them.
