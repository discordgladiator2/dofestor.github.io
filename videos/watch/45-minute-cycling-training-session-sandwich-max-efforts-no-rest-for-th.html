Today’s session is a tough one, 45 minutes in length with a mixed variety of aerobic threshold efforts and short intervals at high intensity - this is going to get your heart pumping! This session is ideal to do once a week for a longer hard workout, but bear in mind you’ll need a good recovery afterwards!
