Instead of comparing the kingdom of God to something grand like the cedar tree, Jesus likens it to a mustard seed. The Church is filled with both saints and sinners. And though imperfect, through the mystery of God, it will grow — just as how the Church grew from a seemingly motley band of apostles. So, no matter how small our acts or contributions seem to be, they are llike seeds, which play a part in the growing of the kingdom.
