Balupu is an upcoming Telugu film directed by Gopichand Malineni. The film is being produced by Prasad Vara Potluri under PVP Cinemas banner and stars Ravi Teja, Shruti Haasan and Anjali in lead roles.The film soundtrack is being composed by S. Thaman. The film will have a Telugu and Tamil release in May 2013.
