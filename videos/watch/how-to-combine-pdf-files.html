How To Combine PDF Files?
————————-
There are a lot of ways to combine two PDF file into one. But I’ll show you two best methods to do the same.

METHOD - 1 (Using Google Drive)
——————————-

Step 1. Open your Google Drive.
Step 2. To combine the PDF, We need to connect "PDF Mergy" app to our Drive.
Step 3. Click on New - More - Connect more apps.
Step 4. Now search "PDF Mergy" in the search bar of the dialog box.
Step 5. Click on "Connect" to connect the app to our Google Drive & close the dialog box.
Step 6. Now select any two PDF files which you want to merge/combine.
Step 7. Right click - open with - PDF Mergy.
Step 8. Click on "Grant access to the Google Drive".
Step 9. Select your account and click allow.
Step 10. Now click on "Merge" to merge the files.
Step 11. Your file is ready now. you can save it to your computer or to the Google Drive.

METHOD - 2 (Using PDF Creator - Third-Party Tool)
————————————————-

Step 1. Download and Install "PDF Creator" software from its official website.
Step 2. Open PDF Creator and drag or choose the two pdf files which you want to merge.
Step 3. First, give the name to your new merged file and choose the file location to save then Click on "Merge".
Step 4. Click on "Merge All" and "Continue".
Step 5. Click on "Save" to save the merged PDF file.
That’s all.
