This painting is done on a cotton fabric using Camel Fabrica acrylic paint.

Colors used
White
lemon yellow
Golden yellow
scarlet red
crimson
Emerald green
Sap green
Prussian blue
Black

Brushes
No 2 andb6 flat brush
No 0 round brush
Filbert brushes No 2



Thin paint with right amount of water to prevent spread on fabric and to keep it as soft as possible after painting

Iron on opposite side after 24 hours and wash after one week to fix colors.

Hand painted fabric care -
Dry clean and iron on opposite side.
Always try to hang hand painted fabrics on a cloth hanger
