1. COMPERSSOR.
The most essential component of any air conditioning system is the compressor. Your nice, cool air depends on it! The four main functions that your vehicle‚Äôs compressor carries out include:

Your vehicle‚Äôs A/C condenser is usually located in the front of the radiator, giving it the nickname of the mini-radiator. In addition, the condenser is also responsible for moving the cooled liquid refrigerant to the receiver/dryer or accumulator.

4. EXPANSION VALVE.
Thermal Expansion Valve or Orifice Tube should be located between the condenser and evaporator. Together, they monitor the amount of pressure and temperature of your A/C system and calculate the exact amount of refrigerant that can safely go into the evaporator.
