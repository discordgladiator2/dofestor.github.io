Hi everyone,today im going to share you a scramble egg style of cooking a very healthy food and a lot of benefit to our body. here’s my preparation below for this recipe.

PREPARATION:

4 eggs
2 Tomatoes
Onion
Salt
white pepper
Chives
Olive oil

First, clean the onion and cut in circle shape
Second,clean tomatoes and cut same with the onion then cut each into half
Third take a pan brush with olive oil and put the tomatoes and Onion in the middle
Fourth Clean and cut the chives leaves into small pieces set aside
Fifth take a bowl break 4 eggs and add the chives leaves the mix well
Sixth heated the pan with tomatoes and onion add salt and white pepper the turn the other side then add the mixture of eggs add some salt and white pepper again. covet for 3 minutes in low heat once done add little salt pepper the your scrambled eggs done and ready for meals.
